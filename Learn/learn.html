The Basics

Investment

Investment is the idea of allocating resources with the intention of profiting in the future. However, there is always a chance to lose out on your investment. Therefore, to invest responsibly, one should try to minimize risk as much as possible. Ultimately, investing requires balancing risk and reward to reap the most profits.

Stocks

Stocks represent ownership of a company. Purchasing stocks is equivalent to buying partial ownership of said company. Those who hold stocks in a company are known as the company's shareholders. Shareholders can profit off of dividends(distribution of profit to shareholders) or stock price increases(increasing the value of the stock).

Stock Market

The stock market is the means for stocks to be traded between investors and companies. Companies come to the stock market to publicly sell shares of ownership, while investors come to buy and sell shares of ownership in hopes for gaining profits. The stock market can be unpredictable, so there is always a level of risk involved.
